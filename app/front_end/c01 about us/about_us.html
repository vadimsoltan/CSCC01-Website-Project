OUR STORY

Integrow

"Integrow" is the combination of "intelegence" and "grow". By Gorky, "the book is the ladder of the progress of mankind", which agrees with our idea to let people have more intelegence and wider view via reading. The wealth of people is inequal, while the right to access knowledge is not. Thus we aims to build a book exchange platform to offer a choice of books with lower price but not lower value for reading lovers.
