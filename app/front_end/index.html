Welcome to Integrow Book Shop

Intergrow provides a platform for its users where they can sell and buy both, used and new books. Integrow thrives from its own growing community of sellers who provide a large selection of different titles. Are you looking to sell your books from previous semesters or are you looking for new ones for an upcoming year?

Start looking for the books you need at the prices you want with Intergrow.

Update your profile

By creating an account with Integrow you will be able to post your information and interact with users that have similar interests. You will also be able to make posts for books you wish to sell and bookmark posts you liked.

Make a post

Looking to sell a book? Make a post about it! You can make a post with your account on Integrow and let other users search for it in our online book shop.

My own posts

On Intergrow you can manage your posts right here. Need to update an older posting? Sold a book and need to delete another one? Like a post and want to keep it? You can do all that and more right here. .
